AI Multimedia News Production

AI-generated video, audio, images and digital humans for news

What is AI Multimedia News Production?

AI multimedia production is a revolutionary process that applies generative models to the automation of visual and audio news productions and editing. This encompasses a broad scope of outputs that AI can produce including images, video clips, voiceovers, subtitles, infographics, and even digital news anchors. With the combination of these technologies, newsrooms may save a lot of time and money in their production and produce more interesting and accessible content to a wide range of audiences on the websites, mobile applications, and social media.

This is usually done by transforming the text based news stories into multi-sensory experiences. As an example, a news story on economic trends can be automatically converted into a video of a short video with moving charts and a computer-generated voiceover. This does not only make user engagement, but also simplifies complex information. This technology is taking the place of conventional digital journalism as observed in the work of the Chinese media such as Xinhua and Caixin.

Traditional vs AI Multimedia Production

Traditional Workflow

Needs a group of photographers, videographers, designers, editors, and voice actors. The cost is high, production is slow and hard to scale. Lack of visual support in many text stories is because of a lack of resources.

AI-powered Workflow

AI translates text to video, auto-generates subtitles, generates images, synthesizes voice, and clips. Multi-format packages can be filled in by one individual within minutes, making multimedia production democratized.

Main Applications in Modern Newsrooms

AI Voice & Podcasts

Maximum Natural voice synthesis of news audio and multilingual dubbing. This enables the news to be reached by non-native speakers and the visually impaired.

Text-to-Video Briefs

Short social media news videos can be generated with one-click and are best placed on such platforms as Tik Tok and Instagram Reels, and they reach younger audiences.

AI Digital Anchors

Full time virtual news anchors to do 24/7 broadcasting particularly during an event happening overnight or when information has to be updated in other languages.
